Science Centre

Welcome to Glasgow Science Centre, an award-winning visitor attraction located on the banks of the river Clyde! Our mission is to inspire and engage people of all ages with science and technology, and to promote the importance of STEM.


Whether you are a science enthusiast or just looking for a fun and educational day out, we invite you to come and discover all that Glasgow Science Centre has to offer.


At Glasgow Science Centre, we are dedicated to making science accessible to everyone, regardless of age, background, or ability. We offer a wide range of educational programs and resources for schools and families.
